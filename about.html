About Us


Hello! We are Sporganizer, or sports + organizer. We make it easy for you to plan events or meets for your sports team in public parks. With our website and soon-to-be application, the process is faster than ever! Unlike Evite or other competitors, we include a messaging system that allows users to collaborate on when and where the events are. Sporganizer: we organize so you don't have to!
